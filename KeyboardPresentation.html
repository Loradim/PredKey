What is Predkey?

  • PredKey is an innovative and improved keyboard
  • PredKey predicts the next word in the sentence and gives the top three suggestions. With a click/keypress you accept the suggested word without fully typing it
  • PredKey works both with ngrams (looking the the words typed before) and taking into account the partial word the user already has typed
  • PredKey uses a dictionary of 20,000 most common english words
  • PredKey's algorithm is easy to extend (e.g. learn new words, new ngrams the user often uses)

Performance of PredKey

How much can you improve when using PredKey?


  • 1,000 blog entry samples: avg 42.9 % less keypresses

  • 1,000 news article samples: avg 44.54 % less keypresses

  • 1,000 twitter tweets samples: avg 41.35 % less keypresses
